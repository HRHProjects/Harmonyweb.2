## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does account approval take?**
A: Usually 1-2 business days. You'll get an email when approved.

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes! All data is encrypted. Never upload sensitive IDs without our request.

**Q: Can I upload large files?**
A: Max 10 MB per file. Contact us for larger files.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the sign in page.

**Q: How do I logout?**
A: Click "Sign out" in the top right corner of any page.

**Q: Can I delete my files?**
A: Contact our team to manage your uploads.
